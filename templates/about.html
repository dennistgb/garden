Empowering Green Thumbs, Automatically

The Automated Gardening System is your smart companion for a flourishing garden. It takes the guesswork out of plant care by continuously monitoring and adjusting essential environmental factors.

Key Features:

  • Real-time monitoring of pH, temperature, humidity, light, and EC levels.
  • Intuitive dashboard with graphs for easy data visualization.
  • Customizable settings to optimize for your specific plants.
  • Automatic alerts and adjustments for a worry-free gardening experience.
